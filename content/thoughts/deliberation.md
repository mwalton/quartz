## Deliberation tools
- [Pol.is](https://pol.is/): is a tool for listening to the public at scale to locate the area of rough consensus on a given issue. It is a real-time system for gathering, analyzing and understanding what large groups of people think in their own words, enabled by advanced statistics and machine learning.
